About Me

I am a very curious person who loves acquiring new knowledge and skills, and also a passionate problem solver with knowledge on several areas.

My formal education is on computer science, however, I am self-taught on several artistic disciplines, including digital and traditional il·lustration, music theory and composition and videogame design and development. I can also play bass and keyboard, and plan to eventually learn guitar as well.

I have made a videogame. It started as my research project for batxillerat, but I enjoyed making it so much that I kept working on it long after I finished the project. It served as an excuse for me to learn many new skills, such as color theory, 2-d animation, sound design, musical composition and many more. Also, the fact that I have been iterating upon it so much means that more than once I have had to fix and clean up chuncks of messy code I wrote when I was still a novice programmer, winch means I trully understand the importance of clean and readable code.
